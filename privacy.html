1. Introduction

Mantra Mala Jaap ("the App") is a spiritual counting application designed to help users track their mantra practice using an interactive digital mala. This Privacy Policy applies to all versions of the App, including mobile applications for iOS and Android.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Mantra Mala Jaap is designed to work completely offline. We do NOT collect any personal information from you. The App does not require:

  • User registration or login
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Name or personal identification
  • Payment information (for free version)
  • Location data
  • Contact information

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App does NOT collect any automatic information such as:

  • Device identifiers (IMEI, IDFA, etc.)
  • IP addresses
  • Browsing history
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

2.3 Data Stored Locally

All data related to your mantra counting practice is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Daily count records
  • Lifetime statistics
  • User preferences and settings
  • Selected mala type
  • Mantra selections
  • Vibration and sound preferences

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we have no information to use, share, or sell. The App operates entirely offline with all data remaining on your device.

4. Data Security

4.1 Local Storage Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features:

  • iOS: Data is stored in the app's sandbox directory, protected by iOS security mechanisms
  • Android: Data is stored in the app's private directory, protected by Android security mechanisms

4.2 No Network Transmission

The App does not transmit any data over the internet. All information remains on your device at all times.

4.3 Data Deletion

You can delete all your data by:

  • Using the "Reset All Data" option in the App's Settings screen
  • Uninstalling the App (all local data will be deleted)
  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
