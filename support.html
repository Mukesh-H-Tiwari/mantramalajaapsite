‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Mantra Mala Jaap free?

A: Yes! Mantra Mala Jaap is completely free to download and use. The app includes all core features at no cost. Some premium mala types may be available through optional in-app purchases.

Q: Does the app require internet?

A: No! Mantra Mala Jaap is completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. You don't need an internet connection to use the app.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes! We do not collect any personal information. All your counting data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: How do I reset my data?

A: You can reset all your data by going to Settings ‚Üí Reset All Data. This will delete all your counts and statistics. This action cannot be undone.

Q: Can I backup my data?

A: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can backup your data using your device's built-in backup features (iCloud for iOS, Google Drive for Android).

Q: How many beads are in the mala?

A: The mala has 108 beads, which is the traditional number used in spiritual practices. You can drag the beads to count your mantras.

Q: What mantras are available?

A: Mantra Mala Jaap includes 18+ mantras in multiple categories including Lord Shiva, Lord Vishnu/Krishna, Lord Ganesha, Goddesses, Hanuman, and Universal mantras. All mantras are available in both English and Hindi.

Q: Can I change the mala type?

A: Yes! You can choose from 7 different mala types: Rudraksha, Tulsi, Crystal, Sandalwood, Gold, Emoji, and Neon. Go to the Mala Store to select your preferred mala.

Q: How do I disable vibration?

A: Go to Settings and toggle "Enable Vibration" off. You can also adjust the vibration intensity using the slider.

Q: How do I disable sound?

A: Go to Settings and toggle "Enable Sound" off to disable sound effects.

Q: Can I set a custom lifetime target?

A: Yes! Go to Settings and enter your desired lifetime target. The default is 1,000,000 but you can set any number.

Q: What is the streak counter?

A: The streak counter shows how many consecutive days you've counted mantras. It resets if you miss a day.

Q: Can I see my statistics?

A: Yes! Go to the Stats screen to see your lifetime count, average count, best day, current streak, and a 30-day chart.

Q: Does the app work on both iOS and Android?

A: Yes! Mantra Mala Jaap is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play Store) with identical features.

Q: What is the minimum iOS version?

A: Mantra Mala Jaap requires iOS 12.0 or later.

Q: What is the minimum Android version?

A: Mantra Mala Jaap requires Android 6.0 (API 23) or later.

Q: Is the app suitable for children?

A: Yes! Mantra Mala Jaap is appropriate for users of all ages, including children. The app does not collect personal information, contains no ads, and is completely safe for children.

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: If you encounter any issues, please email us at bytecode.creation@gmail.com with details about the problem.

üêõ Troubleshooting

App Crashes on Launch

  • Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app
  • Make sure your device has enough storage space
  • Update your device's operating system
  • Clear the app's cache (Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Mantra Mala Jaap ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Clear Cache)

Vibration Not Working

  • Check that vibration is enabled in Settings
  • Make sure your device's vibration is enabled in system settings
  • Test on a physical device (simulator doesn't support vibration)
  • Restart the app

Sound Not Working

  • Check that sound is enabled in Settings
  • Make sure your device's volume is not muted
  • Check your device's volume settings
  • Restart the app

Data Not Saving

  • Make sure you have enough storage space on your device
  • Try restarting the app
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app (note: this will delete your data)

Beads Not Responding

  • Make sure you're dragging the beads downward
  • Try restarting the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update the app to the latest version
